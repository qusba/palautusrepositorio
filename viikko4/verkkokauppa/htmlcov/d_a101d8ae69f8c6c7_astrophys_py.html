86# ENERGY 

87 

88# Here, explicitly convert the planck constant to 'eV s' since the constant 

89# can override that to give a more precise value that takes into account 

90# covariances between e and h. Eventually, this may also be replaced with 

91# just `_si.Ryd.to(eV)`. 

92def_unit(['Ry', 'rydberg'], 

93 (_si.Ryd * _si.c * _si.h.to(si.eV * si.s)).to(si.eV), 

94 namespace=_ns, prefixes=True, 

95 doc="Rydberg: Energy of a photon whose wavenumber is the Rydberg " 

96 "constant", 

97 format={'latex': r'R_{\infty}', 'unicode': 'R∞'}) 

98
